SMART HOME SOLUTION

Improve your smart life experience, provide complete systems including audio visual, lighting control, home automation and smart security, etc ...

INTRODUCTION

In order to enhance the smart life experience of users, ORVIBO provides De-house smart home solution.

This solution includes 7 smart systems: Smart lighting, smart security, central condition, appliances control, access control, smart curtain and energy management.

FEATURES

  • Simple Installation. No wiring when use the ZigBee wireless technology.
  • Convenient. More user-friendly design and interaction.
  • Secure Cloud. Reliable smart home cloud platform.
  • More Compatible. Support to link with smart devices of different brands.
  • Low Power Consumption. ZigBee communication is stable and economic

SMART OFFICE SOLUTION

Smart lighting and energy system for office. More energy-saving office solution and more efficient office management, save 30% energy cost.

INTRODUCTION

Green Office solution is designed for joint office, small and medium enterprises. It is a smart lighting and energy management system.

With the current protection and electricity usage monitoring function, it provides users the more convenient, comfortable and secure smart life.

FEATURES

  • 24-hour remote control and management.
  • Monitor the energy consumption by departments, districts and dates.
  • Automatically adjust the lighting and central A/C as per the environment.
  • Data recording and analysis by the cloud server, provide targeted energy-saving advice for companies.
  • User-friendly management backstage, raise the efficiency.

SMART HOTEL SOLUTION

Integrate systems including Lighting, sun-shading and curtains, audio visual, air conditioning, etc.. Provide efficient, user-friendly and comfortable hotel experience.

INTRODUCTION

WeHotel is an innovative and smart management system for hotel, which is developed based on the latest IOT and mobile Internet technology.

WeHotel system can monitor and control devices locally and remotely, optimize operation procedure and save energy, so that the total cost on both management and operation will be reduced.

FEATURES

  • Monitoring and controlling devices remotely.
  • Easy and simple check-in via Wechat or User App.
  • Comprehensive room's status information.
  • Various smart scenes: sleeping mode, welcome mode, all lights off mode, check-out mode etc.
  • Wireless room control system, easy to install, modify and maintain.
